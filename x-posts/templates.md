# 📋 X Post Templates & Strategy

> Templates for daily/weekly X posts to build in public.  
> Consistency > Perfection. Post every day, no matter what.

---

## 🎯 Posting Strategy

- **Daily post:** What you learned + what you built (keep it short)
- **Weekly thread:** Deep-dive on the week's biggest topic
- **Monthly mega-thread:** Progress update with screenshots, stats, learnings
- **Engagement:** Reply to 5–10 Web3 accounts daily, add value

## 💡 Engagement Tips

1. **Tag relevant accounts** when sharing learnings about their protocol/tool
2. **Reply to popular Web3 accounts** with genuine, technical insights
3. **Share your struggles** — people connect with authenticity
4. **Quote-tweet** interesting articles with your own take
5. **Create polls** about Web3 topics to boost engagement
6. **Share code snippets** as images (use Carbon or Ray.so)
7. **Be consistent** — the algorithm rewards daily activity
