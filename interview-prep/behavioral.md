# 🗣️ Behavioral Interview Prep

## STAR Method Template
- **S**ituation: Context and background
- **T**ask: What you needed to accomplish
- **A**ction: What you specifically did
- **R**esult: The outcome and what you learned

## Common Questions

### Motivation
1. Why are you interested in Web3/blockchain?
2. What excites you most about decentralized systems?
3. Where do you see Web3 in 5 years?

### Experience
4. Tell me about a challenging project you built.
5. How do you approach learning new technologies?
6. Describe a time you had to debug a difficult issue.

### Collaboration
7. How do you handle code reviews?
8. Describe your experience with open source.
9. How do you stay current with blockchain developments?

### Problem Solving
10. Walk me through how you'd audit a smart contract.
11. How do you prioritize security vs speed of development?
12. Describe a time you had to make a technical trade-off.
